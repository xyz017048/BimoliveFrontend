Register

 Click "Sign up" button to sign up.

 Fill out all the information.


Student: Watch Live

After logged in.

Click "live" to watch all the live streaming.

Or at the top navigation bar, click "Learn", in the submenu, select "LiveNow" to watch all the live streaming.


Student: Watch Replay

After logged in.

Click "Replay" to watch all the available replay videos.

Or at the top navigation bar, click "Learn", in the submenu, select "Replay Videos" to watch all the available replay videos.


Student: Follow Course and Teacher

After logged in.

Choose one of the live videos or replay videos.

On the top right corner, click "Follow Teacher" or "Follow Course".


Student: Search

After logged in.

On the top right corner, there is a "search textbox".

Type keyword, and click the "search icon" to search.

Search for teacher's name or course's name


Student: Apply to Teach

Go to your profile, before apply to teach.

Two ways to go to your profile.

On the top right corner

First, click your "account name", in the submenu, select "Account Setting"

Second, click your "profile picture".

Now, in your account setting page.

On the top of middle of the page.

Click "Apply to teach".

Fill out the form, and click "Submit" button to submit the form.


Teacher: OBS Set Up(Windows)

Go to https://obsproject.com , download OBS for windows.

Open the OBS, and click "Setting" button.

On the setting page.

Select "Broadcast Settings".

On the "Broadcast Settings" page, put "rtmp://52.33.149.173/live" into "FMS URL".

On the "Broadcast Settings" page, put "key, which is in your account setting page" into "Play Path/Stream Key(if any).

Click "OK" to save the setting.

Finally, click "Start Streaming". and "Start Recording" (if you need to upload your video lately, saving record as mp4 format).


Teacher: OBS Set Up(Mac)

Go to https://obsproject.com , download OBS for mac.

Open the OBS, and click "Setting" button.

On the setting page.

Select "Stream".

On the "Stream" page, put "rtmp://52.33.149.173/live" into "URL".

On the "Stream" page, put "key, which is in your account setting page" into "Stream Key".

Select "Output".

On the "Output" page, choose a "Recording Path".

On the "Output" page, choose "mp4" for "Recording Format".

Click "OK" to save the setting.

Finally, click "Start Streaming". and "Start Recording" (if you need to upload your video lately, saving record as mp4 format).


Teacher: Create Course and Lecture

On the navigation bar, click "Teach", select "My Courses".

Click "Cross Icon" to add a course.

Fill out the form, and click "Create" button to create a course.

Click the course that just created.

Choose "START A LECTURE" or "Cross Icon" to create a lecture


Teacher: Start Lecture

After added a lecture.

Click one of the lectures.

In the lecture page, click "START LECTURE" button to start a lecture.


Teacher: Upload Replay Video

After end the lecture, you can go ahead and upload the record video via click "Upload Replay Video".
